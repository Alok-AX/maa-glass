Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our glass products and services.

We offer a wide range of glass types including tempered glass, laminated glass, frosted glass, mirror glass, tinted glass, and decorative glass. Each type serves different purposes and comes with various customization options.

For custom glass orders, measure the width, height, and thickness required. Always measure in multiple spots as openings may not be perfectly square. Our team can also provide professional measurement services if needed.

Standard lead times are 7-10 business days for most products. Custom or specialized glass may take 2-3 weeks. Rush services are available for an additional fee with lead times as short as 3-5 business days.

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our glass products. Our certified installers ensure perfect fitting and can handle projects of any size. Installation costs vary based on project complexity.
